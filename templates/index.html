Programming Community of NIT Durgapur

We are programming community of NIT Durgapur, with focus on improving coding culture institute wide by conducting regular lectures from beginner to advance topics of programming. Our goal is to increase student's participation in inter-collegiate contest like ACM-ICPC and help them get better.

WHO ARE WE?

MISSION

Working towards the improvement of campus's coding culture by organizing coding classes,coding contests,geeky sessions and competitive environment

VALUE

We believe that helping each other is the only way t goal. We take care of the community and the people and always look to get the best out of everyone.

Our activities

  • Regular coding classes and geeky sessions
  • RECode : Monthly online coding contest
  • Alohomora : Onsite coding contest
  • Improving student's participation in ICPC
  • REChase : Annual treasure hunting competition
  • Organizing AMA Sessions with successful alumnus
  • Tech Townhall : Annual talk with successful students or alumni
  • Mind Sweepers : Combining logic and concepts with fun and frolic
